

# Bridal Couture Studio - Frontend Plan

## Brand Vision
A **high-end bridal wear website** with an **Ivory & Rose Gold** color palette, featuring **subtle, elegant animations** using Three.js. The design will feel **royal, traditional, minimalist, and aesthetic** — perfect for showcasing premium Indian wedding attire.

---

## Pages & Structure

### 1. **Home Page (Hero Experience)**
- **Full-screen hero** with a stunning bridal image/video and your studio name in elegant serif typography
- **Floating gold particle animation** (Three.js) creating an ethereal, dreamy atmosphere
- **"Discover the Collection"** call-to-action with smooth scroll
- **Featured collections** carousel showcasing Lehengas, Sarees, Anarkalis
- **Atelier philosophy** section — your brand story in minimal text
- **Instagram/Lookbook preview** grid
- **Footer** with contact, social links, and newsletter signup

### 2. **Collections Page**
- **Category navigation**: Bridal Lehengas | Sarees | Anarkalis | Accessories
- **Filter options**: Color, Price Range, Occasion (Sangeet, Reception, etc.)
- **Product grid** with elegant hover effects (zoom, quick view)
- **Subtle fabric-like flowing animation** in the background

### 3. **Individual Product Pages**
- **Large image gallery** with zoom capability
- **Product details**: Name, price, fabric, embroidery details, customization options
- **Size guide** and care instructions
- **"Inquire Now" / "Book Appointment"** buttons
- **Related pieces** section
- **Styling suggestions** carousel

### 4. **Lookbook / Editorial Gallery**
- **Full-screen image experience** — editorial photoshoot style
- **Horizontal scroll** or masonry grid layout
- **Minimal text** — let the visuals speak
- **Three.js subtle parallax effects** on scroll

### 5. **About / The Atelier**
- **Brand story** — your journey, inspiration, craftsmanship philosophy
- **The design process** — behind-the-scenes imagery
- **Meet the founder/designer** section
- **Atelier location** with elegant imagery

### 6. **Contact / Appointment Booking**
- **Appointment request form** — for bridal consultations
- **Store location** with embedded map
- **Contact information** — email, phone, WhatsApp
- **Social media links**

## Design Elements

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Ivory (#FFFEF5) & Rose Gold (#D4A373)
- **Accent**: Soft blush (#F5E6E0), Champagne gold (#D4AF37)
- **Text**: Deep charcoal (#2C2C2C) for readability
- **Background textures**: Subtle damask or paisley patterns at low opacity

### Typography
- **Headings**: Elegant serif font (Playfair Display or Cormorant Garamond)
- **Body**: Clean sans-serif (Inter or Lato)
- **Accents**: Script font for special callouts

### Three.js Animations
- **Floating gold particles** — gentle, dreamy effect on hero sections
- **Fabric-like flowing mesh** — subtle movement mimicking silk
- **Soft glow/bloom effects** — adding a luxurious feel
- **Parallax depth** — on scroll for immersive experience

### Micro-interactions
- **Elegant hover states** — images subtly zoom, buttons glow
- **Smooth page transitions** — fade-in with gentle scale
- **Loading animation** — minimal, elegant spinner or brand mark reveal

---

## Mobile Experience
- **Fully responsive** — optimized for all devices
- **Touch-friendly gallery** navigation
- **Simplified navigation** — hamburger menu with elegant slide-in
- **Fast loading** — optimized images and lazy loading

The structure will be built to easily integrate:
